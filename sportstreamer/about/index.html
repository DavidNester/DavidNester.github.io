About

I love watching sports! Without cable, I generally have to find ways to stream them online. The best way I have found to do this has been through the various forums on reddit. I have gotten tired of sorting through all of the comments and navigating between the bookmarked forums so I decided to make this website. With one bookmark, you can easily switch between sports and get all of the links without sorting through any of the obnoxious comments.

This website gets all of the information from reddit but needs the extension to do it. I did not make the extension but am happy with it for the time being. It has some issues which I will try to fix when I get the chance.

I made this site as a learning experience and also because I felt it had value to me. I have no intention of including ads though I may consider banner ads (not pop ups). I have lots of improvements in mind and they will come as I have time to add them. Check out the donate page for a list of updates!

Happy streaming!

Requirements

  • Desktop
  • Chrome
  • Chrome Extension
  • Soda Player for ace streams (or you can copy them yourself)
