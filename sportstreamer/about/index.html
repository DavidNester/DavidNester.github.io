About

I love watching sports! Without cable, I generally have to find ways to stream them online. The best way I have found to do this has been through the various forums on reddit. I have gotten tired of sorting through all of the comments and navigating between the bookmarked forums so I decided to make this website. With one bookmark, you can easily switch between sports and get all of the links without sorting through any of the obnoxious comments. I do not host any steams. I just compile them in one place without clutter or ads.

I made this site as a learning experience and also because I felt it had value to me. I have lots of improvements in mind and they will come as I have time to add them. Check out the donate page for a list of updates! My goal now is to spread this to as many people as possible!

Happy streaming!
