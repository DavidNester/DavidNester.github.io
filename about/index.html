About Me

My name is David Nester. I grew up in Bluffton, Ohio and went to college at Eastern Mennonite University in Harrisonburg, Virginia. I majored in Computer Science and Mathematics. In August 2018, I will start working as a software developer at Epic Systems in Verona, WI.

I have played soccer ever since I have attended school. I am an avid sports fan and watch all the time (this led to my site SportStreamer). My teams are Arsenal FC and Real Madrid FC. Outside of soccer, I am a major fan of the Ohio State Buckeyes (CFB and BB), Cleveland Browns, and Cleveland Cavaliers.

I also enjoy a lot of tv shows, specifically comedies. My favorites (in a close but not exact order) are Community, Arrested Development, 30 Rock, The Office, Rick and Morty, Parks and Recreation, and Futurama. I have many more that I like but didn't quite make the list.

When I am not watching something, I enjoy being outdoors a lot. I love anything with pedals (uni, bi, tri). I try to use bicycle as my main form of transportation. You can check out my cross country ride here. I also enjoy Ultimate Frisbee, running, and hiking.
